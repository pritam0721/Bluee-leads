## Why Data Quality Matters

In outbound B2B telemarketing, your data is your most valuable asset. Before a single call is made, the quality of your contact list determines whether your campaign will succeed or fail.

Poor data quality leads to wasted agent time, lower connection rates, compliance risks, and frustrated prospects. Conversely, clean, verified data dramatically improves every campaign metric.

## Key Data Quality Factors

- **Accuracy**: Is the contact information current and correct?
- **Completeness**: Do you have all the fields needed to personalise outreach?
- **Relevance**: Does the contact match your ideal customer profile?
- **Compliance**: Has the data been sourced and maintained in line with UK GDPR?
- **Recency**: When was the data last verified or updated?

## The Cost of Bad Data

Industry research suggests that UK businesses lose an average of £14 million per year due to poor data quality. In a telemarketing context, this manifests as:

- 30-40% of calls reaching wrong numbers or ex-employees
- Wasted agent hours on unresponsive or irrelevant contacts
- Increased GDPR compliance risk from outdated suppression lists
- Damaged brand reputation through poorly-targeted outreach
