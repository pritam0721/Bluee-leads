## About the Role

As a Voice-Processing-Associate at Bluee Leads Marketing Limited, you will conduct CATI (Computer-Assisted Telephone Interviewing) surveys, process voice data, and analyze findings to deliver actionable market intelligence for our clients. You'll play a crucial role in gathering and processing voice-based research data that drives business decisions.

## Key Responsibilities

- Conduct outbound CATI (Computer-Assisted Telephone Interviewing) surveys
- Process and analyze voice and survey data with accuracy
- Maintain detailed records of survey responses and data quality
- Deliver clear insights from collected market intelligence
- Support research teams on client projects
- Meet daily and weekly survey targets

## What We're Looking For

- Experience with CATI surveys or voice data collection
- Strong communication and listening skills
- Ability to accurately process and record voice data
- Basic data analysis capabilities
- High attention to detail and accuracy
- Reliability and consistent performance
- Comfortable working with remote teams
